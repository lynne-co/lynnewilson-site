Questions you might have

Do you only work with women-led businesses?

Yes. This is non-negotiable. Lynne Wilson & Co. exists specifically to support women-led businesses and that focus shapes everything about how we work.

Can I start with ad hoc work before committing to a retainer?

Yes, ad hoc work is available at the standard hourly rates. Many clients start this way and move to a retainer once they've seen how we work together.

Do you offer discounts on retainers?

No. Retainers are never discounted. The value of a retainer is guaranteed, direct access to Lynne — not a lower hourly rate. One hour is one hour regardless of volume.

What if I need more than one type of support?

That's very common. Many clients work with me across multiple areas — social media, email and OBM, for example. We'll work out the right combination and package in your proposal.

How quickly can you start?

This depends on current availability. Book a discovery call and I'll be honest about timelines. If there's a waitlist, I'll let you know straight away.

What is yoga nidra and how do I book a session?

Yoga nidra is a deeply restorative guided practice — sometimes called yogic sleep. Sessions are bespoke and available for individuals, teams and retreats. Book via the discovery call link or email directly.
