About Lynne

I built this for the woman who is brilliant at what she does and exhausted by everything else.

The woman behind the agency

I'm Lynne Wilson — founder of Lynne Wilson & Co., OBM, fractional marketing director, social media strategist, email marketer, podcast producer and yoga nidra guide.

That's a long list, and it's intentional. Because the women I work with don't have one problem — they have many. They're running businesses they love, wearing every hat, and somewhere between the strategy sessions and the school run, the marketing has quietly fallen off the list.

I've spent years of experience inside businesses of all shapes and sizes, watching brilliant women get buried under the operational weight of their own success. I know what it looks like when a business is thriving on the outside and quietly chaos on the inside.

That's exactly where I come in.

I bring calm to the chaos. I take the things that are falling through the gaps — the emails not going out, the social content not getting made, the strategy that lives in your head but never makes it onto a page — and I actually do them.

Women-led businesses deserve women-led support. That's not a tagline. It's why this agency exists.

I bring calm to the chaos. In your business and in your nervous system.

"You know you need a strategy. You know you need support. But finding the right people feels like yet another thing on a list that's already too long."

What I believe in

Women-led, always

This agency exists to serve women-led businesses. That focus is non-negotiable. It shapes who I work with, how I work and why I built this the way I did.

Access, not discounts

A retainer isn't a cheaper version of ad hoc work. It's guaranteed, direct access to me. I don't discount retainers because the value is the relationship and the reliability — not the rate.

Calm over chaos

Urgency is a tool that gets overused. I work with a steady, strategic hand. I'm proactive, thorough and I get things done without making you feel like everything is on fire.

Genuine care

I don't just manage tasks — I genuinely care about the success of the businesses I work with. My clients' wins feel like my wins. Their stress is something I actively want to reduce.

Whole-person support

Business support and nervous system support aren't separate things. A woman who is burned out cannot lead her business well. My yoga nidra work sits alongside everything else for exactly that reason.

No fluff

No inflated packages. No unnecessary extras. No jargon for the sake of it. Just clear, honest, effective support that makes a real difference to how your business runs.

The yoga nidra specialism

Alongside the business support, I'm a trained yoga nidra guide. Yoga nidra — sometimes called yogic sleep — is a deeply restorative practice that brings the body and mind into a state of conscious rest.

It's not meditation in the traditional sense. It's more accessible, more immediately effective and — for busy women who can't switch off — often transformative.

I offer bespoke yoga nidra sessions for individual clients, teams and retreats. Each session is crafted specifically for the group or person I'm working with.

Business performance and personal wellbeing are not separate conversations. I believe in supporting both.
