My Story

I left corporate because I knew it was costing me more than it gave me

I was good at it. I knew how to get results, how to build relationships, how to make things happen. But somewhere along the way, the work stopped fitting the person I was becoming.

The day I left, I felt the kind of relief that comes when you've been holding your breath for so long you'd forgotten what it felt like to exhale.

I built Lynne Wilson and Co. because I know what it means to be buried in everything while trying to grow a business. I've lived it. And I've watched too many women in business run themselves into the ground trying to do all of it, all of the time, alone.

"You're wearing all of the hats. The marketer, the admin, the social media manager, the email writer. You know exactly what your business needs. You just can't get to it because you're too busy keeping everything afloat. That's exactly where I come in."

I understand the kind of exhaustion that comes with building something meaningful while managing everything else life asks of you.

My clients get more than a skilled pair of hands. They get someone who is genuinely invested in what they're building, who operates at their level, and who treats their business with the same care they do.

Built in my forties. Started with clarity, not chaos. Every year of experience was preparation, not delay.

What I Believe

The principles that shape how I work

01
Relationship over transaction

I don't take on clients. I build working relationships. There's a difference, and you'll feel it from the first call. I care about what you're building, not just what's on the task list.

02
Expertise through lived experience

I don't recite theory. I've made the mistakes, I learned from them, and now know how to help you avoid them.

03
Rest is not optional

The most important thing I've learned is that sustainable performance requires genuine rest. I'm a yoga nidra and breathwork specialist precisely because I believe women in business deserve to lead without burning out.

04
You shouldn't have to do it alone

Yes, we're superwomen, every single one of us, but that doesn't mean we should have to do it all alone. Knowing when to delegate and reach out for help is a sign of strength. It's leadership.

05
Honesty builds trust faster than polish

I will always tell you what I think, including when I think something isn't working. I'd rather have an honest conversation that moves your business forward than tell you what you want to hear.

06
Your story is your most powerful tool

Not your services, not your prices and not your credentials. The real, human, honest version of why you built what you built. That's what makes people choose you. I'll help you tell it properly.

Beyond the Agency

The part that makes Lynne Wilson and Co. completely different

Most marketing agencies will tell you to work harder, post more, show up consistently. What almost none of them will tell you is that the most important thing you can do for your business performance is rest your nervous system properly.

I'm a qualified yoga nidra, meditation and breathwork specialist. Techniques backed by hundreds of peer-reviewed studies showing significant benefits for focus, decision-making and sustained performance.

I've seen what happens when women in business actually rest. They don't slow down. They speed up. They make better decisions. They lead more clearly. They stop running on empty and start running on something real.

This is why I also founded Rested and Rising, a wellness platform for women in business built around the science of rest. Because the agency work and the wellbeing work come from exactly the same place. You can't pour from an empty cup, and I'm not interested in helping you fill your schedule if it comes at the cost of your health.
